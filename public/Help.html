Help Center

1. Register

Click Register on the Home Page and fill in your details:

  • Full Name
  • Registration Number ( e.g. BEEE/22/SS/013)
  • Phone Number
  • Password
  • Upload your Student ID/The most recent photo of yourself

After successful registration you will be redirected to the confirmation page and can proceed to booking.

2. Booking a Seat

Click Book, select your destination, and login. You will then choose whether to book for yourself or another passenger. After that:

  • Upload your proof of payment (mobile money screenshot)
  • Select your preferred available seat on the bus
  • Confirm — your booking will be marked as pending

3. Payment & Verification

Pay the booking fee of K5,000 via mobile money, then upload your receipt. An administrator will review and approve your booking. This is a booking fee to secure your seat, and its part of your transport fare.

4. Viewing Seat Status

Click View Available Seats on the Home Page, login, and see the live bus layout. Hover over any seat to see who has booked it.

5. Admin

Administrators will assess proof of payment and approve/reject payment .
